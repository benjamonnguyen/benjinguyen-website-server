Pomomo is a Discord bot inspired by a need to balance productivity and socializing. It uses the proven Pomodoro technique which alternates periods of work and relaxation to maximize productivity while minimizing burnout.

Designate time to focus so you and your friends can get stuff done while hanging out! The intervals are customizable to whatever works best for you.

Pomomo is now in over 25k servers and has been internationalized into French, Dutch, Spanish, English, and Italian, thanks to the contributions from our community of 'Pomomies'!
